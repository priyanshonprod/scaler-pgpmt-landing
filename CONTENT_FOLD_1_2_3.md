# Voice & Tone Rules (apply to all 3 folds)

1. **Opportunity-framed, not fear-framed.** AI = leverage, not threat. The live narrative is explicit on this — match it.
2. **Specifics > adjectives.** "63% of employers" beats "many employers." "₹8,400/month, no-cost EMI" beats "affordable." "200+ live hours" beats "comprehensive."
3. **Operator language.** *Spec, ship, evaluate, deploy, capstone, P&L, force multiplier, AI orchestration.* Not *unlock, transform, journey, reimagine.*
4. **Earn every claim.** If you say "leaders," show 8 logos. If you say "data," show the chart. If you say "mentor," name them.
5. **Confidence without arrogance.** The reader is a senior professional. Speak peer-to-peer.
6. **One emotion per fold.** Fold 1 = recognition. Fold 2 = opportunity. Fold 3 = confidence + commit. Don't dilute.
7. **Cut adverbs and hedge words.** "Carefully designed" → "designed." "Truly transformative" → delete. "Could potentially help you" → "helps you."

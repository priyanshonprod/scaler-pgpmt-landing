Why upskill now?

The bar moved. The roles moved. The salaries moved. You either move with them, or you get managed by people who did.

01
All roles are changing across seniority. From IC to CXO — every job description being written in 2026 has an AI layer baked in. The shift is not coming for you. It already arrived.
02
Growth is assured if you understand AI and adopt it. AI-fluent professionals are getting promoted 2–3× faster, with median compensation hikes of 40–100% across functions.
03
AI knowledge and right usage in day-to-day work will set you apart. The differentiator is no longer who knows AI exists — it's who has actually deployed it on a real business problem.
04
Leadership skill with AI knowledge is the hottest need of the hour. Companies are not short on AI engineers. They are short on leaders who can decide what to build, where to deploy, and how to measure impact.

The questions worth asking before you decide.

Is this an MBA? +
No. This is a Post Graduate Program in Business & AI — a practitioner credential. If you want a formal MBA credential, the IIM-Trichy executive certificate covers it as part of the program.
I work full-time. Can I manage this? +
Designed for it. Weekend live sessions (Sat–Sun mornings). 4–6 hours async during the week. All recorded. Most Cohort 1 enrollees are in senior roles at large companies.
How is this different from traditional PG programs? +
Three things. The cohort: working professionals, 2+ years experience minimum. The curriculum: built around 2026 AI, not a 3-year-old program with an AI module bolted on. The instructors: practitioners working in the field today.
How is this different from a self-paced online course? +
Self-paced platforms are great for self-study. What they can't provide: practitioner mentorship, accountability from a cohort of senior peers, a structured career path, and a capstone with a real sponsor company. You're not paying for content — you're paying for a network and a credential.
I don't have a deep tech background. Is this for me? +
This is not a coding program. It's for professionals across business functions — product, operations, finance, sales, strategy — who need to understand AI well enough to make decisions about it and lead teams that use it. No coding experience required.

We don't guarantee promotions. But: AI-skilled professionals earn 40–100% more depending on function. Every capstone is a live deliverable for your promotion conversation. The cohort network is the most lasting return for many.
